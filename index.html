The Pilot Session

The Pilot Session is a single full day event we are running in order to kick off the Hack Lab. It will be an awesome day of building and meeting other like minded young people.

When is it?

We hope to be able to bring this pilot session to you sometime in early March or April. Once we have a date set we will be sure to push it out to all of our subscribers on our mailing list.

Who is it for?

This event is targeted at people who have not graduated High School. It is not just for beginners though, and will be catering for every skill level! If you are in University or beyond and would like to get involved email Harrison (harrison [at] thehacklab [dot] xyz).

What exactly is happening?

In all honesty, we don't completely know yet. However, we do have a few ideas for things we want to happen.

  1. Workshops for beginners
  2. Panel with industry mentors
  3. Talks from awesome people
  4. Oculus Rift and other technologies of the future demos

The one thing that we know will happen is learning. If you have any ideas that you would like to share, get in touch!

Club Meetings

Starting in early May we will be running weekly meetings for students to come attend as they wish. These will essentially a smaller version of the Pilot day, and should be thought of like any other after school club
