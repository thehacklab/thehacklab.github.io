Our Mission

At the Hack Lab we are creating the community we wished existed when we were learning to code. We are bringing together young people interested in technology from all across Sydney and providing an environment for them to learn in whatever way they wish. It is our belief that a proper programming education should be available to everyone.

For each session we bring together a team of passionate mentors and an interesting host of new technologies for our members to mess around with.

Pilot (mini-hackathon)

To kick start our awesome community we are putting on a short 8 hour hackathon to show the world what we are all about. Sign up to be kept up to date.

You can find more information about this on the event's page.

Club Meetings

Starting in early May we will be running weekly meetings for students to come attend as they wish. These will essentially a smaller version of the Pilot day, and should be thought of like any other after school club or activity.
