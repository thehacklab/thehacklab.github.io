What do we do?

Imagine a community where youth interested in tech can come and learn together. A safe place for all genders, races and abilities to do the thing they are passionate about. They can learn anything from web development, game development, app development, hardware hacking... Anything. That is what we do.

How do we do it?

We have an awesome team of mentors and teachers who run curriculum every week at a special location in the Sydney CBD.
