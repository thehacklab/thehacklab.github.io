Our Mission

We at the Hack Lab are creating the community we wished we had when we were learning to code. Basically, we are bringing together a bunch of talented mentors, high schooler programmers (of all skill levels), and cool technology. It is our belief that a proper programming education should be available to everyone.

Pilot (mini-hackathon)

In order to kick start our awesome community we are putting on a short 8 hour hackathon for prospective club members to get a taste of what we are all about. Sign up to be kept up to date.

You can find more information on the event's page.

Club Meetings

Starting in early May we will be running weekly meetings for students to come attend as they wish. These will essentially a smaller version of the Pilot day, and should be thought of like any other after school club
